Configuration and Deployment

Database Fields

Discussion of what constitutes a database field in GPD and how to use them.

Patient and Treatment Templates

Patient and Treatment Templates allow the user to quickly enter a collection of standard fields into database records.

Analysis

GPD allows you to see relevant information from multiple treatments in a chart (table) or graph, so that trends or correlations may be spotted more easily.

Customizing PDF Treatment Reports

GPD can generate PDF reports of treatment graphs and summaries. This guide describes the relevant options in Preferences.

HL7 Functionality

Details the preferences and commands available for working with HL7

Markers

A marker is a user-defined indicator that something has occurred at a certain point during data collection, for instance that treatment was administered, the seizure ended, or an artifact was present.

Finalizing Treatments

Often a user will be required to transmit the results of a treatment, in one form or another, to the hospital EMR system, and GPD's finalization functions provide a method to mark certain treatments as transmitted, aka finalized.

Patient Summary Reports

Complex reports can be generated from patient and treatment data using a sophisticated templating language.
